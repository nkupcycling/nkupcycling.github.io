About N&K Upcycling

We are Niamh and Ksenia, “Transition Year Students”. We have based our company on the idea of Up-Cycling. This means we have taken every day objects and re-purposed them for new uses. For example a jam jar is transformed into a glitter vase or a wine bottle into a dinner candle holder. We hope you like our range of products which are reasonably priced and are helping to save the environment.

If you would like to buy any of our products please get in touch with us by emailing nkupcycling@gmail.com. We are based at Pipers Hill College but can also arrange local delivery in the Naas, Rathmore and Sallins areas.

Thank you for visiting our page.

"Upcycling is designed to work in opposition to consumer culture, encouraging people to think of new and innovative ways to use things, instead of simply buying new consumer goods. It also benefits the environment, by promoting reuse over discarding whenever possible."
